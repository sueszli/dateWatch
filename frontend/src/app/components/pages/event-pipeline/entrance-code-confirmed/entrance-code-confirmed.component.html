How it works

At the event, you’ll either be seated by the host or have to find a date yourself.


Then one of you both has to enter the other persons username into their phone.


You then have to wait until the host starts the timer for all participants.



The timer determines how much time you get on to get to know your date.


After the timer runs out - you'll have to decide whether you liked your date.



After the event you’ll get your matches on this app and get a reminder via mail.
